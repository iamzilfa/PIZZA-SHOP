ABOUT US

At Oh la la Pizza , we put dietary diversity first because we believe that everyone deserves to eat great pizza. Our vegan crust is devoid of any animal products, and serves as the base of our pizza.

We’re proud to have such a wide variety of vegan options available, and we’re always expanding our menu to include more options for everyone.

Not only do we have vegan choices, but our dietary alternatives extend to gluten free and vegetarian too.
